    • Daily COVID-19 Cases Analysis in 2021

    Malaysia reported a total of 2,147,675 COVID-19 Cases in 2021 with several infection waves. In this year, cases have reached all time highs multiple times, with the highest number of cases being 24,599 on the 26th August. Ever since February, Malaysia has been ramping up its vaccination progress, and has undergone lockdown for a total of three times.

    Where are these cases coming from?

    Despite having several total lockdowns in Malaysia, our cases does not seem to fall. This might be explained by the fact that work places such as factories are still allowed to operate, even during the lockdown period. Without surprise, majority of the cases fall within that category.

    What is the vaccination progress in Malaysia?

    Ever since 24th of February, the Malaysian Government has started its mass vaccination program. Up to this date, we can see that more than half than the population of Malaysia has been vaccinated as of 30th of September. We are projected to reach 80% immunity in November. It can be seen that the Malaysian Government has increased the daily vaccination amount since June, up to July.
