    Malaysia reported a total of 2,147,675 COVID-19 Cases in 2021 with several infection waves. In this year, cases have reached all time highs multiple times, with the highest number of cases being 24,599 on the 26th August. Ever since February, Malaysia has been ramping up its vaccination progress, and has undergone lockdown for a total of three times.
